# CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

- **Be respectful**: Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- **Be collaborative**: Work constructively with others.
- **No harassment**: Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination is not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Instances of harassing behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Maintainers retain the right to remove, edit, or delete comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that they deem harassing, abusive, or otherwise inappropriate.
